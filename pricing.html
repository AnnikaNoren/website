Pricing

The InspireDo program is priced to provide the greatest value in the test preparation market place. The summer session which runs from the beginning of July to the beginning of October, has a program fee of $1,900 per student. In addition to the program fee, each student is required to obtain a Muse headset (~$300), an Android smart device (~$50), and two ACT Prep books (~$50 total). The Muse headset, Android smart device and ACT books must be purchased prior to the start of the program. The InspireDo session fee is broken into two payments: $950 is due on August 1st and the remaining $950 is due on October 1st.
