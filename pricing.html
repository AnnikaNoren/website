Pricing

The InspireDo session is $1,900 per student. In addition, each student is required to purchase a Muse headset (~$300), an Android smart device (~$50), and two ACT Prep books (~$50 total). The Muse headset, Android smart device and ACT books must be purchased prior to the start of the program. The InspireDo session fee is broken into two payments: $950 is due on August 1st and the remaining $950 is due on October 1st.



Money Back Guarantee

InspireDo guarantees that you will find the program to be helpful. If at any time prior to December 1, 2016 you find that the program did not offer $1,900 worth of value, InspireDo will refund the difference between the value you paid and the value you believe you received.
