Program Cost Details

The charge for the InspireDo session will be based on whichever test (ACT or SAT) yields the greatest score improvement. The session charge is due after official test scores have been received and the score improvement can be determined. In addition to the session cost, each student will need to purchase a Muse EEG headband (about $300). If at the end of the session, the student’s family no longer wishes to keep the Muse and the Muse is in good condition, InspireDo will re-purchase the headband from the student. The Muse is able to be used by other family members.


Each student’s baseline ACT score will be the Composite Score from taking an official ACT practice test at the beginning of the session. The Composite Score is the average of the 4 core ACT sections (Math, English, Reading and Science).


Each student’s baseline SAT score will be determined by using the official ACT to SAT concordance table set to be released in May of 2016.
